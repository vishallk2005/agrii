    About AgriDirect

    AgriDirect is an online agricultural marketplace connecting farmers directly with customers. Our mission is to empower farmers by providing them with a platform to showcase and sell their produce, while giving customers access to fresh and affordable products straight from the source.

    With categories like vegetables, fruits, and flowers, we make shopping simple, direct, and transparent. By reducing middlemen, we ensure fair pricing for farmers and high-quality fresh produce for customers.

    Together, let’s build a sustainable agricultural community and support our farmers.
